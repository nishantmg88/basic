Principal's Message

Words of wisdom from our leader

Mr. Sanu Kanchha Tamang

Principal

M.Ed, B.Ed | 20+ Years Experience

"Welcome to Shree Sharada Basic School, where we believe every child is unique and capable of achieving greatness. Our mission is to provide quality education that not only focuses on academic excellence but also nurtures the overall personality development of our students. We are committed to creating a learning environment that encourages curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. Our dedicated team of teachers works tirelessly to ensure that each student receives personalized attention and guidance. At our school, we emphasize the importance of values, discipline, and respect for others. We prepare our students not just for examinations, but for life itself, making them responsible citizens of tomorrow. I invite you to join our school family and be part of this wonderful journey of learning and growth."

Mr. Sanu Kanchha Tamang
Principal
